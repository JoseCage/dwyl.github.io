We are a community of people

passionate about using technology to solve real problems.

What do we do?

We build Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) that test ideas as fast as possible.

If you have an idea for a digital product or service, we would love to hear from you!

How do we do it?

We have a 3-step approach to getting your idea(s) tested in the hands of real people.

  1. Understand the problem that needs to be solved and confirm the intended users feel the pain and need for a solution.
  2. Prototype the solution and get feedback from real people as fast as possible.
  3. Review customer feedback and confirm we are making progress toward solving the problem.

What do we believe in?

Fully Tested Code

We're proud of the quality of our code. Having tests means that when we add to that code we know we're not creating a problem somewhere else.

Simplicity

We keep it simple and intuitive, with only the features you need and a tried and tested user journey.

Open Source

We're big advocates developing our code our in the open, allowing us to tap into a global network of experts contributors.

Ethics Driven

We people who are doing amazing things and having a positive effect on the world. If that's you, we'd love to hear from you!
